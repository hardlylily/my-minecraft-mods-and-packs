my most used minecraft mods in a github repo to share to my friends

Installation:
Click on the green "Code" button and then on "Download zip", extract and copy the folders in your .minecraft directory.

If you're installing this to play on my server, shaders and resourcepacks are optional, as well as most mods

List of required mods: 
TRMT (The Roads More Travelled),
Fabric API,
Fabric Language Kotlin,
Silk,
Pandalib,
Falling Trees,
Veinminer and Veinminer enchantment,
WorldEdit,
Architectury
